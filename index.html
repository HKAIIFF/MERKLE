The Merkle Proof
Technology. Merkle 默克爾樹
證明技術詳解

We use Merkle Trees to cryptographically prove your inclusion in the distribution list without storing massive data on-chain. It is the gold standard for whitelist verification. 我們使用默克爾樹（Merkle Tree）以密碼學方式證明您在分發列表中的資格，無需在鏈上存儲海量數據。這是白名單驗證的黃金標準。

Your wallet address & amount (Leaf Node) are hashed up the tree. The final "Root" is stored on the Solana blockchain. To claim, you only need to provide the path (Proof). 您的錢包地址和數量（葉節點）經過層層哈希向上計算。最終的「根哈希」存儲在 Solana 區塊鏈上。領取時，您只需提供路徑（證明）。

1

Cryptographic Certainty 密碼學確定性

A Merkle Tree works like a digital fingerprint. If even one byte of data (e.g., your token amount) changes, the Root Hash changes completely. Since the Root is locked on-chain, your data is impossible to tamper with. 默克爾樹就像數字指紋。如果任何一個字節的數據（例如您的代幣數量）發生變化，根哈希就會完全改變。由於根哈希已被鎖定在鏈上，您的數據不可能被篡改。

2

Privacy & Efficiency 隱私與效率

Instead of publishing everyone's data publicly, we only store the Root. You only need to prove *your* branch to the smart contract to claim tokens, maintaining efficiency and privacy. 我們不公開發布所有人的數據，只存儲根哈希。您只需向智能合約證明*您的*分支即可領取代幣，既高效又保護隱私。

3

Trustless Verification 去信任化驗證

The verification logic is executed by the Solana program, not a central server. Code is law. If the math matches, the funds are released. 驗證邏輯由 Solana 程序執行，而非中心化服務器。代碼即法律。只要數學計算匹配，資金就會自動釋放。

Immutable不可篡改

Once your wallet and amount are hashed into the Merkle tree, no one can modify it. 一旦您的錢包和數量被哈希到 Merkle 樹中，任何人都無法修改。

Non-revocable不可撤銷

Official cannot revoke your rights, and you cannot cancel purchase. 官方無法撤銷您的權益，您也無法取消購買。

Auto-Execution自動執行

Tokens automatically released to your wallet according to contract rules. 代幣將根據合約規則自動釋放到您的錢包。

Trustless去信任化

Rely on code, not human operation. Eliminating human error risks. 依賴代碼而非人工操作。消除人為錯誤風險。
